# 1. Follow-Up Page – Lead Details Cannot Be Opened

## Current Issue
The leads displayed in the **Follow-Up** module are not clickable.

Users should be able to open the complete Lead Details panel exactly the same way as they can from:

- Dashboard
- Lead Listing
- Kanban Board
- Analytics
- Search Results
- Notifications
- Everywhere else a lead is displayed

Currently clicking the lead does nothing.

This breaks navigation consistency.

---

## Expected Behaviour

Clicking anywhere on the lead row (or at minimum the Lead Name) should:

- Open the Lead Details drawer/panel
- Preserve all existing functionality
- Allow editing (based on permissions)
- Show Notes
- Show Timeline
- Show Documents
- Show Followers
- Show Activity History
- Show Assignment
- Everything exactly identical to opening the lead from other modules.

No duplicate implementation.

Reuse the existing Lead Details component.
